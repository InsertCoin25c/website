HOW TO BUY $25C

Step 1

Create MetaMask Wallet

Create a MetaMask Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile device. That will allow you to buy, sell, send, and receive $25C.

Step 2

Send $ETH to MetaMask

Transfer $ETH to your new MetaMask wallet from your existing wallet (e.g. Trust Wallet, Coinbase, or Binance), or buy $ETH directly within MetaMask.

Step 3

Connect To UniSwap

The safest place to buy $25C will be UniSwap soon. Visit UniSwap and click “connect wallet” MetaMask will ask for your signature, go ahead and sign it.

Step 4

Swap $ETH For $25C

Set your slippage to 3-6% and enter and the amount of $ETH you would like to swap for $25C. Your $25C will now be in your MetaMask wallet.
